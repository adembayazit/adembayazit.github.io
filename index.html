Why I Created This Microblog?

First of all, I want to clarify that I am not building a social media platform. I have only allocated a small area for myself on my platform to share my own opinions.

Perhaps in the future, it could evolve into a social media platform. (Supply-Demand Eq)

I am developing the platform from the ground up, completely on my own. I am not simply taking an open-source application and modifying it like others do.

Furthermore, I needed a core idea for the platform. Here it is: I have shared the photos, which are the fundamental building blocks of the idea. An entirely new platform is being built upon this foundation.

So, what am I sharing here? On this platform, I am not voicing something unknown. In fact, I bring to light what already exists but slips past your attention.

Also, the entry space on this platform was born out of necessity.

It concerns Technology, Art, Photography, the World, and the reinterpretation of developments in world history.

At the same time, it contains a cryptological and steganographic language and art.

Friends, I'll soon start filling in the content. As the ideas form, they gradually take shape. I assure you, it will include knowledge that has never surfaced before. Since there's no team working in the background and I'm progressing entirely with personal effort and open-source development tools, it takes a little time.

As I mentioned in the text, the cryptology and steganography content will be created in real time. The artworks, on the other hand, will feature the works of old masters such as Picasso, Da Vinci, Salvador Dalí, and many other talented artists whose names you may not have heard of. I will also reinterpret them with photographs by adding my own techniques.
